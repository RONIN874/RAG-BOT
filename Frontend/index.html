PMTPT Clinical AI

Ask your TB database
in plain English.

PMTPT Clinical AI lets healthcare workers and programme managers query the national Tuberculosis Preventive Treatment database without writing a single line of SQL. Type a question, get a clinical answer.

What you can do

Patient Tracking

Look up active enrolments, treatment status, adherence rates, and follow-up records by district, facility, or patient.

Regimen & Drug Info

Query 6H, 3HP, 1HP, 3HR regimens — dosing, duration, contraindications, drug interactions, and adverse event thresholds.

Programme Indicators

Generate care cascade reports, completion rates, interruption counts, and monitoring indicators per district or facility.

Supply Chain

Check drug stock levels, batch details, expiry status, and replenishment data across facilities.

This tool queries the PMTPT database using AI-generated responses based on India's 2021 NTEP guidelines. It is intended for programme management and reporting use. Always verify critical clinical decisions with the treating physician.
