How it works

Upload PDFs

Upload any PDF — guidelines, protocols, lab reports, or research papers. Documents are indexed automatically.

Semantic Search

The AI searches your documents by meaning, not just keywords — finding relevant passages even when phrased differently.

Plain English Answers

Ask in natural language. The AI synthesises answers from the relevant document sections and cites the source page.

Multi-document Support

Upload multiple PDFs across categories. The AI searches across all indexed documents to find the most relevant answer.

Answers are generated from the content of your uploaded PDF documents only. The AI does not have knowledge outside of what has been indexed.
